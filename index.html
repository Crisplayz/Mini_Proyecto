Is the Xiaomi Redmi Note 8 a good phone?

smartphone from Xiaomi that offers plenty of power for those who are operating on a budget. The hardware that Xiaomi has included for around US$165 is remarkable.

camera

The Xiaomi Redmi Note 8 has a 13MP f/2.0 selfie camera, and the focus is fixed as usual. ... The 13MP selfies we shot are excellent.

Impressive battery life
